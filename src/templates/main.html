Paris Climate Agreement

The Paris Agreement's long-term goal is to keep the increase in global average temperature to well below 2 K above pre-industrial levels; and to limit the increase to 1.5 K, since this would substantially reduce the risks and effects of climate change.
An increase of 2 K is related to 450 ppm CO2 - concentration.
